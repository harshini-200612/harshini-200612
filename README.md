# 💫 About Me:
🔭 I’m currently working on<br>An advanced Multimodal Ovarian Cancer Detection system that integrates medical imaging and genomic data. This project leverages the Swin Transformer for hierarchical feature extraction from imaging modalities like ultrasound and MRI , alongside DNABERT to process complex DNA sequences for high-accuracy diagnosis.<br>👯 I’m looking to collaborate on<br>Projects at the intersection of Bioinformatics and Computer Vision, specifically those focusing on multimodal data fusion (radiomics + genomics) to enable precision medicine for oncology.<br>🤝 I’m looking for help with<br>Accessing larger, validated clinical datasets to improve model generalization and addressing the interpretability challenges of deep learning models in a clinical setting.<br>🌱 I’m currently learning<br>Transformer architectures (like Swin Transformer), genomic language models (DNABERT), and methods for integrating multi-omics data (genomics, proteomics, and metabolomics).<br>⚡ Fun fact<br>While early AI models for ovarian cancer achieved around 80% accuracy , the latest fuzzy deep learning and multimodal approaches are now reaching accuracy rates as high as 92.99%
